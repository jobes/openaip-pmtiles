OpenAIP PMTile Generator

Worldwide Aviation Database & Tile Export Utility

About the Project

Welcome to the Worldwide PMTile Generator for openAIP, the free worldwide aviation database. This utility processes crowd-sourced aeronautical information into highly efficient, single-file .pmtiles archives, suitable for offline mapping and high-performance web applications.

Generation Exceptions

To maintain optimal processing speeds and prevent tile size bloat, data generation is currently disabled for the following regions:

Argentina FIR COMODORO
Australia MELBOURNE FIR CTA A2
Greenland NUUK SECTOR NORTH

* These regions are excluded due to performance degradation during the tiling process. Including them increases the two hour generation process to more than 6 hours and produces tiles many times larger than the rest of the world.
